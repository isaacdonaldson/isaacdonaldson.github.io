Battlesnake 2019

Battlesnake is a competition in Victoria that is held by sendwithus. Competitors program an AI snake and compete against each other. This was my first year competing and We entered in the beginner category as a team of 3. We placed top 12 in beginner and learned a lot of lessons. You can find a link to the competition here https://play.battlesnake.io/.
