Nim Wiki

Nim Wiki is a website I created to give Nim developers a short domain name to use for their libraries homepages. I saw it as an opportunity to give back to the community without having to be a contributor to an open source project. There are no current users beside someone I asked to help me test it, but I also have not advertised it to anyone.
